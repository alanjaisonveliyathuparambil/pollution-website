SOIL POLLUTION

INTRODUCTION

Soil pollution occurs when harmful chemicals, waste, and toxic substances mix with the soil, making it infertile and dangerous for living organisms.

1. What is soil pollution?
Soil pollution is the contamination of soil with harmful substances that reduce its quality and productivity.

2. How does it destroy our lives?
It reduces crop growth, contaminates food chains, and affects human health through toxic substances.

CAUSES

Soil pollution is mainly caused by industrial waste dumping, excessive use of pesticides and fertilizers, plastic waste, and chemical leakage.

How to Prevent Soil Pollution?

Soil pollution can be reduced by using organic farming methods, reducing plastic waste, proper waste disposal, and avoiding harmful chemical fertilizers.
