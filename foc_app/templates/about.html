What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into our environment. It affects air, water, soil, and noise levels, making life unhealthy for humans and nature.

Air Pollution
Water Pollution
Noise Pollution

Why This Matters

Pollution damages health, destroys ecosystems, and affects future generations. Awareness is the first step toward protecting our planet.
