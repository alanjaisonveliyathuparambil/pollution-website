WATER POLLUTION

INTRODUCTION

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances such as chemicals, waste, and plastics enter water bodies like rivers, lakes, and oceans.

1. What is water pollution?
Water pollution is contamination of water resources that makes them unsafe for drinking and living organisms.

2. How does it destroy our lives?
It spreads diseases, destroys marine life, and affects the availability of clean drinking water.

CAUSES

Water pollution is mainly caused by industrial waste, sewage discharge, plastic dumping, and agricultural chemicals entering water bodies.

How to Prevent Water Pollution?

Water pollution can be reduced by treating sewage, reducing plastic usage, avoiding dumping waste into rivers, and using eco-friendly chemicals.
